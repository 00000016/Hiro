🌳 Understanding "Self-Reliance" Read

My explanatory essay on Ralph Waldo Emerson's "Self-Reliance" essay. As I saw it, despite being Emerson's most famous work, the "Self-Reliance" essay was almost completely misunderstood both by the common reader and the academic. This essay was an effort to correct that and explain the soul of "Self-Reliance"
